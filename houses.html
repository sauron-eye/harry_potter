Volume II · The Houses

The Four Houses

Founded over a thousand years ago by the greatest witches and wizards of their age — each house a philosophy, a family, a destiny.

G 🦁
Gryffindor
Nerve · Courage · Chivalry
Founded by Godric Gryffindor · c. 993 AD

Where dwell the brave at heart. Their daring, nerve, and chivalry set Gryffindors apart. Those who dare to face their fears, who would stand between their friends and any darkness — this is their home. The house values courage in all forms, and despises cowardice above all else.

Bravery Chivalry Nerve Daring Determination
ColoursScarlet & Gold
ElementFire
GhostNearly Headless Nick
S 🐍
Slytherin
Ambition · Cunning · Resourcefulness
Founded by Salazar Slytherin · c. 993 AD

Those of serpentine subtlety, who use any means to achieve their ends. Slytherin's house embodies cunning and resourcefulness — not mere villainy, but a ferocious will to succeed. Many great wizards emerged from this house, and the greatest Muggle-born protectors wore silver and green.

Ambition Cunning Leadership Resourcefulness Self-preservation
ColoursEmerald & Silver
ElementWater
GhostThe Bloody Baron
R 🦅
Ravenclaw
Wit · Learning · Wisdom
Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw · c. 993 AD

Those of wit and learning will always find their kind here. Ravenclaws prize intelligence, creativity, and the endless hunger to understand. The brilliant eccentrics, the philosophers, the inventors — here they are honoured, not mocked. The mind is the greatest magic of all.

Intelligence Wisdom Creativity Originality Acceptance
ColoursSapphire & Bronze
ElementAir
GhostThe Grey Lady
H 🦡
Hufflepuff
Loyalty · Patience · Fair Play
Founded by Helga Hufflepuff · c. 993 AD

Those patient souls who value fair play and kindness — who stay when others flee, who work when others give up. The most underestimated house produced Nymphadora Tonks, Cedric Diggory, and statistically the fewest dark wizards of any house. Do not mistake kindness for weakness.

Loyalty Hard Work Fair Play Patience Dedication
ColoursYellow & Black
ElementEarth
GhostThe Fat Friar
